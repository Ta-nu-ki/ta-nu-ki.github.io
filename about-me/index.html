About me

My name is Katya Bondar. I identify myself as a performance artist, alien, choreographer, cyborg, witch, Russian, fluid, error. I consider reality in terms of escaping of being a system creation. I haunt my ghosts. In my artworks, I use hybrid forms of space to shift the reality to a new humanity. This approach helps to question the veracity of what is seen, what is transmitted and to deconstruct the existing historical paradigms. For me, performance is a space where tradition is impossible, or, I mean that new form is a territory of the repression of the old tradition. I'm a performance artist, because performance is a place of truth. It's a place where everyone can live along with the unconscious.
