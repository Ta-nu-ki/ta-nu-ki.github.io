Presentation of Heat and Humidity solo

work-in-progress

The Club of Anonymous Dancing, Nekrasov Library, Moscow, Russia – 28 December 2019

How can you articulate a problem into a dance? How to create conditions for movement to be born? How to move in a disaster?

In my work, I examine physicality from the point of view of the political aspects of gender, queer, sexuality, historical trauma, identity. I’m interested in today issues related to the theme of thermal disaster, black holes, future, slime, hybrid space.

  • Katya offers a number of hashtags to her solo work:
  • heat and humidity
  • slowness infection
  • movement attaches
  • tongue = anti-current / glue
  • tomorrow melts in statistical losses
  • sensory coma
  • constant occurrence or intense compression
  • slow-caking translation of memory into momentum
  • neutralization of thermal characteristics cancels thermal guidance

The Club of Anonymous Dancing is an evening of presenting new works in progress by performance artists. At meetings, the viewer is offered the practice of watching - LOVING GAZE. With the help of this practice, organizers want to create an adoption space that reveals the potential of each of the participants. For a loving look, it doesn’t matter: do you have a description ready for work, do you concretize your statement, do you fully understand what the concept is - it’s all not important! If you find yourself in a situation of “ignorance” - be sure to come to us! We want to get away from the conventional framework for creating work and focus on the process and collective experience on a sensual level.

  • When: 12/28/2019, 6:00 p.m. - 9:00 p.m.
  • Where: conference room of the Nekrasov library. (Baumanskaya St., 58/25, p. 14; 5th floor)
  • Showing 4 works with a maximum timing of 20 minutes + 10 minutes to discuss each work.
