Murder Girl

choreography

Ploschadka 8/3, Free Theater, Moscow, Russia
15 February 2020

I’m happy to announce that I will participate as the choreographer in the Murder Girl performance. This performance is a part of the Murder Girl multimedia project by Diana Galimzyanova that is currently composed of short film and videoblog. Performance is based on the incident in the life of a completely ordinary serial killer blogger. Genuine sayings of real maniacs from different eras and countries will be used in the performance.

  • Where: Ploschadka 8/3, Free Theater (Kazakova str. 8/3, Moscow, Russia)
  • When: 15 February 2020, 8PM - 9PM
