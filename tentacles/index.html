Tentacles

performance

NAKT Festival, Hamburg, Germany — 9 November 2019

/hi//what does this body makes?//

From the perspective of hyperverities, chthonic “abominations with many tentacles (polytendriled abominations)” - “Unspoken” (Unuttera) that awaits us at the end of the story, is leading the story. The tentacles of these hyperbelieving abominations extend through time, are selected to the present moment, revealing themselves as the “dark will” of progress: it breaks political cultures, destroys traditions, dissolves subjectivity. Hypervision is a framework or channel into which everything apocalyptic flows.

/people//what is this mind made of?//
