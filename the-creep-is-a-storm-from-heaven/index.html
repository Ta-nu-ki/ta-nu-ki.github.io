The Creep Is A Storm From Heaven

performance

MMOMA Petorvka 25 /Moscow Museum of Modern Art/, Moscow, Russia — 15 April 2019

You are falling into the dead space. You are washed by the waves of emptiness. The signal is lost. You become vulnerable. Vulnerable means to be visible to other people. To be visible to others means to destroy yourself from the inside and outside.

You cannot stop what cannot be stopped. You cannot touch without being touched.
